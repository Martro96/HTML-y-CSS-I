ABOUT ME

I have 4 years of experience in Photography and 2 in Graphic Design. Visual design and the Beauty of Things have always accompanied me on every step in my life. I felt the need to bridge the gap I had between form and function, and that led me to UX/UI and there I found my element. Right now I am serving as UX/UI Teacher Assistant, position that allowed me to reinforce my skills and develop a personal workflow. Mentoring has also given me the chance to help other designers-to-be to explore and find their own voice and create awesome designs.
